Our Vision

At Evaara Resorts, we envision redefining luxury through a harmonious blend of nature, serenity, and personalized experiences. Our goal is to create a haven where modern comforts meet the timeless beauty of Munnar's misty landscapes.

We are committed to sustainable practices that preserve the natural splendor of our surroundings while providing our guests with innovative, guest-centric experiences that leave lasting impressions. Every aspect of our resort is designed with intention, balancing luxury with environmental consciousness.

The Munnar Experience

Munnar's lush tea plantations, cool climate (averaging 15°C), and serene atmosphere create a magical backdrop for your stay at Evaara Resorts. At 3000 feet elevation, you'll experience the perfect balance of comfort and adventure.

Explore nearby attractions including sprawling tea estates, cascading waterfalls, and scenic hiking trails. The region's biodiversity offers nature enthusiasts countless opportunities to discover exotic flora and fauna unique to the Western Ghats.
