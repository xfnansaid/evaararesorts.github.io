Cancellation Policy

Our terms for cancellations and refunds

Cancellation Policy

Effective as of April 07, 2025

1. Reservation Cancellations

At Evaara Resorts, we understand that plans can change. Please review our cancellation policy below:

  • Free Cancellation Period: Cancellations made 7 or more days prior to the check-in date will receive a full refund of the deposit amount.
  • Late Cancellations: Cancellations made 3-6 days prior to the check-in date will incur a charge equivalent to 50% of the total reservation amount.
  • Very Late Cancellations: Cancellations made within 48 hours of the check-in date will incur a charge equivalent to 100% of the first night's stay.
  • No-Shows: Guests who fail to arrive on the scheduled check-in date without prior notification will be charged the full amount of the reservation.

2. Modification of Reservations

Modifications to existing reservations are subject to availability and the following conditions:

  • Requests to change dates must be made at least 3 days prior to the original check-in date.
  • If the modified reservation falls within a higher rate period, the guest will be required to pay the difference.
  • Shortening the length of stay within 3 days of arrival may result in a cancellation fee.

Important Note: During peak seasons (December 20 - January 10, and major holidays), special cancellation policies may apply. Please inquire at the time of booking for specific terms.

3. Refund Process

When a refund is approved according to our cancellation policy:

  • Refunds will be processed to the original method of payment.
  • Processing time for refunds is typically 7-14 business days, depending on your financial institution.
  • All refunds will be issued in the original currency of the reservation.

4. Special Circumstances

We understand that emergencies happen. In case of:

  • Medical Emergencies: With proper documentation (such as a doctor's note), we may offer more flexible cancellation terms.
  • Natural Disasters: In the event of natural disasters or severe weather conditions that prevent travel, we will work with guests on a case-by-case basis.
  • Travel Restrictions: If government-imposed travel restrictions prevent your visit, please contact us to discuss your options.

5. Group Bookings

For reservations of 3 or more rooms:

  • A 50% non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking.
  • Full payment is due 14 days prior to arrival.
  • Cancellations made 30 or more days prior to arrival will receive a 50% refund of the deposit.
  • Cancellations made less than 30 days prior to arrival will not be eligible for a refund.
